## Accelerate Your Outreach

Transform your CRM from a static database into a high-velocity sales machine. App Connect provides the tools to automate the "busy work" of calling, so your reps can focus on what they do best: closing.

### Core Capabilities

* **[Click-to-Dial Later](../users/calldown-lists.md#from-click-to-dial):** Don't get distracted by making calls while you're prospecting. As you browse your CRM or LinkedIn, use "Click-to-Dial Later" to queue up prospects into a dedicated outbound list to be tackled during your "Power Hour."
* **[Call-Down Lists](../users/calldown-lists.md):** Streamline your workflow by organizing your queued calls into a continuous list. Once one call ends, the next lead is ready for youâ€”reducing the "between-call" friction that kills momentum.
* **[One-Click Voicemail Drop](../users/voicemail-drop.md):** Stop reciting the same pitch 50 times a day. With one click, drop a pre-recorded, professional voicemail while you immediately move on to the next live prospect.
* **[SMS Templates](../users/sms.md#managing-sms-templates):** Follow up a missed call with a personalized text in seconds. Use templates to ensure consistent messaging while maintaining a high volume of touchpoints.


!!! tip "**The Power of the &quot;Power Hour&quot;**"
    Based on productivity research from the American Psychological Association, separating analytical research from call execution can increase output by up to **40% by eliminating the cognitive costs of task-switching**.[^1]
