## Your Management Command Center
Stop waiting for end-of-week reports to find out how your team is performing. App Connect brings "Floor Management" into the digital age, giving you the tools to see, hear, and improve every interaction.

### **Core Capabilities**

* **[Live HUD (Heads-Up Display)](../users/hud.md):** See the real-time status of your entire team. Know at a glance who is on a call, who is available, and who has been idle for too long.
* **[Company-Wide Metrics](../users/user-report.md#organization-level-call-statistics-and-reports):** Access high-level dashboards that aggregate call volume, duration, and outcomes across your entire organization. Identify peak activity times and resource gaps instantly.
* **[Agent-level Performance Tracking](../users/user-report.md):** Compare individual performance against team benchmarks to identify coaching opportunities or celebrate top producers.

## Empower Your Leadership

Supervision shouldn't feel like "Big Brother"—it should feel like a support system. App Connect 2.0 makes it easy to be the manager your team needs.

1.  **Monitor:** Use the HUD to stay pulse-checked on team activity throughout the day.
2.  **Intervene:** Grab calls languishing on a call queue, or call park location.
3.  **Optimize:** Use the 2.0 Analytics dashboard to adjust your strategy based on hard data.
4.  **Scale:** Standardize excellence by identifying and replicating the workflows of your top 10%.
