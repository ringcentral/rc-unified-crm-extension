    App Connect Solutions

    One Extension. Infinite Possibilities.

    RingCentral App Connect isn't just a dialer; it is a high-performance integration framework designed to bridge the gap between your conversations and your business data. Whether you are looking to accelerate sales, automate data entry, or gain deep AI insights, we have a solution built for your workflow.

    Explore by Business Need

    • Sales Velocity & Outbound Efficiency

      Accelerate your outreach and eliminate the "between-call" friction that kills momentum. Use Call-Down lists and Voicemail Drop to transform your CRM into a high-speed sales engine.

      Learn about Sales Velocity

    • Embedded Communications

      Work where you live. Bring the full power of the RingCentral phone directly into any browser-based application, eliminating tab-switching and the "Toggle Tax."

      Learn about Embedded Tools

    • Compliance & Data Integrity

      Ensure your CRM is always the Single Source of Truth. Automate call and SMS logging with intelligent, unattended conflict resolution rules that keep your data clean without interrupting your team.

      Learn about Auto-Logging

    • Artificial Intelligence

      Turn "dark data" into strategic assets. Use AI Assistant and ACE (AI Conversation Expert) to automatically summarize calls, detect sentiment, and create follow-up tasks. Plus App Connect into any LLM.

      Learn about Conversation AI

    • Supervision & Monitoring

      Manage your team in real-time. From the Live HUD to "Listen, Whisper, Barge" coaching tools, get the visibility you need to scale excellence across your organization.

      Learn about Supervision

    • The Universal Connector

      Stop waiting for custom integrations. Use our developer-friendly JSON manifest framework to embed RingCentral into any proprietary or niche web application in minutes.

      Learn about Custom Integrations

    Why Leading Teams Choose App Connect

    The Challenge The App Connect Solution
    Disconnected Tools Fully embedded UI within your CRM or browser.
    Inaccurate CRM Data Automated server-side and unattended logging.
    Slow Ramp-Up Times Ensure App Connect is configured to same for all users in your company from a centralized location.
    Proprietary systems App Connect provides a developer framework that makes it easy to integrate with proprietary systems that no one else supports.
